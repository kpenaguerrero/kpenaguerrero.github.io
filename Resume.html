About Me

Kaylee Peña Guerrero is expected to graduate from the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO) with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) on December 18, 2026. Her concentrations are in Marketing and Management Leadership, and she has a minor in Communication Studies. She currently serves as a Theatre Office Assistant at the UNO Theater Department and as a Multimedia Communications Intern at the College of Information Science and Technology (CIST) at UNO, where she creates detailed content and explores new ways to streamline business processes. She is a proud recipient of the Volunteer of the Month for April 2024 award by the Maverick Food Pantry, her school’s community-serving pantry. ​ She is grateful to be a Susan Thompson Buffet foundation (Thompson Learning Community, TLC) scholar and a Partnership for Kids foundation (P4K) student. Kaylee has been described by her peers as resourceful, consistent, empathetic, and personable.
